
# Curry Beans

## Ingredients
* 2 tins **baked beans**.
* 1 *chopped* **onion**.
* 1 *chopped* **tomato**.
* 1 tbsp **curry powder**.
* 1/4 tsp **cumin** (*jeera*).
* 1 tbsp **sugar**.
* Knob of **butter**.
* **Salt & pepper** to taste.

## Method
1. *Fry* **onion** in **butter**.
2. *Add & mix* **curry powder & cumin**.
3. *Add* **tomato, baked beans** & remaining ingredients.
4. *Cook* through till done.

## Notes
* If using a *pan*, 1 tin of baked beans works just fine.
