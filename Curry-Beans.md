
# Curry Beans

## Ingredients
* Knob of butter.
* 1 chopped onion.
* 1 tablespoon curry powder.
* 1/4 teaspoon cumin (jeera).
* 1 chopped tomato.
* Salt & pepper to taste.
* 2 tins baked beans.
* 1 tablespoon sugar.
* Cook till done.

## Preparation
* 

## Method
1. Fry onion in butter.
2. Add:
  * Curry powder
  * Cumin
  * Tomato
